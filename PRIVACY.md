# Privacy Policy

**Clipboard Image to Terminal** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- **No telemetry.** The extension and CLI send nothing to any server.
- **No network access.** Everything runs locally on your machine.
- **Clipboard images** are read only when you trigger the command, and are saved
  only to your local output directory (default `/tmp/clipboard-images`). Files
  older than 7 days are pruned automatically. Nothing leaves your device.

That's it. Your clipboard, your machine, your files.
